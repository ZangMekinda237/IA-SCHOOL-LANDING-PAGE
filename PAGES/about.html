About IA School

Excellence in Education Since 1995

Our Mission

To provide a world-class education that empowers students to become critical thinkers, creative problem-solvers, and responsible global citizens.

Our Vision

To be the leading educational institution that shapes the future through innovative teaching, cutting-edge technology, and unwavering commitment to student success.

Our Values

Excellence, Integrity, Innovation, Respect, and Community - these core values guide everything we do at IA School.

Our Story

Founded in 1995, IA School has been at the forefront of educational excellence for nearly three decades. What started as a small institution with 50 students has grown into a comprehensive educational community serving over 2,000 students from kindergarten through high school.

Our journey has been marked by continuous innovation, from being one of the first schools to integrate technology into the classroom to pioneering project-based learning methodologies that prepare students for the challenges of the 21st century.
