Frequently Asked Questions

What are the school hours?

Regular school hours are 8:00 AM to 3:00 PM, Monday through Friday. Extended care is available until 6:00 PM.

How do I apply for admission?

You can apply online through our admissions portal or contact our admissions office at (555) 123-4568.

What extracurricular activities are available?

We offer a wide range of activities including sports, arts, robotics, debate, and community service programs.

Is transportation provided?

Yes, we provide bus transportation to most areas within a 15-mile radius of the school.
